In Ubernet, we are using tokens. You can see the amounts of tokens that you currently have on navbar. Whenever you want, you can add more tokens with our Paypal integration.
You can split a ride with your friends. Everybody gets to pay the same amount of money. If you invite your friends to split fair, they will get email notification. Your ride will be ordered when everyone agrees to split fair.
If you want to reserve a ride, you can do it up to 5 hours in the future.
